Student News October 15, 2025
Coffee with a Cop

The Criminal Justice Society’s “Coffee with a Cop” event offered a welcoming space for students and local law enforcement to connect over casual conversation and shared interests. Held in a relaxed setting with fresh coffee and light refreshments, the gathering encouraged open dialogue about community safety, criminal justice careers, and the human side of policing. Officers shared personal stories and answered questions, while students gained valuable insights into law enforcement perspectives. The event fostered mutual understanding, broke down barriers, and highlighted the importance of collaboration between future justice professionals and those currently serving.

Announcement October 13, 2025
Technology & Justice Studies Day

Technology and Justice Studies Day 2025 was a vibrant celebration of innovation, problem-solving, and career exploration, featuring four immersive sections that highlighted the intersection of tech and justice. In the Game Development & AI zone, students demonstrated interactive projects that blended storytelling with machine learning, showcasing how artificial intelligence can enhance digital experiences. The Drone Flight Safety Challenge tested precision and coordination, as participants navigated obstacle courses while learning about FAA regulations and ethical applications. Cybersecurity sessions delved into real-world threats and defense strategies, with students simulating breach scenarios and discussing data protection careers. Meanwhile, the Criminal Investigation area offered hands-on forensic activities, from fingerprint analysis to digital evidence tracing, bridging technology with investigative techniques. The event empowered students to apply their skills, engage with professionals, and envision future roles in rapidly evolving fields.

Student News October 5, 2025
RSU hosts Inola students

Students from Inola High School recently visited the Rogers State University campus for an engaging exploration of technology and justice, diving into hands-on experiences that bridged innovation with real-world applications. The visit introduced students to foundational coding concepts, artificial intelligence demonstrations, and interactive sessions on digital forensics and ethical tech use. Through guided activities and conversations with RSU faculty and students, the group gained insight into how emerging technologies like AI are reshaping criminal justice, from predictive analytics to cybersecurity. The event sparked curiosity, encouraged critical thinking, and highlighted exciting academic and career pathways in tech-driven justice fields.

Announcement September 19, 2025
Roger State Welcomes Back Oklahoma Military Alumni

Rogers State University proudly welcomed back the Oklahoma Military Academy (OMA) in a spirited tribute that honored its legacy and enduring impact. The event began with a dramatic helicopter drop-off, capturing the attention of students, faculty, and alumni as it touched down on campus grounds. The atmosphere was charged with excitement and reverence as a ceremonial blank cannon firing echoed across the hill, symbolizing tradition, discipline, and pride. This powerful display marked not only the return of OMA but also a celebration of its historical roots and continued influence on leadership and service within the RSU community.

Announcement September 10, 2025
Active Attack Events Training Course

Rogers State University Campus Police recently organized a hands-on active shooter response training designed to prepare students, faculty, and staff for real-world emergency scenarios. The immersive session included situational awareness drills, tactical movement exercises, and guidance on lockdown procedures, empowering participants to respond swiftly and safely under pressure. Officers demonstrated best practices for communication, coordination, and decision-making during high-stress incidents, emphasizing the importance of preparedness and teamwork. This proactive initiative reflects RSU’s commitment to campus safety and ensures the community is equipped with practical knowledge to protect themselves and others in critical situations.
